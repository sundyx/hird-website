About the drone

An autonomous multi-rotor drone with visible and thermal imaging capabilities.

Taking Care of the UAS

HIRD lives inside of our included weatherproof ground station. This enclosure ensures safe, turnkey takeoff and landing to start each inspection pass.

While the drone is not flying, the ground station automatically handles data transmission and charging procedures. 120 watts of onboard PV solar power means it can be placed anywhere on your pasture.

Notifications

SMS feature to send updates after every drone surveillance flight. This includes pictures of stray cattle detected and a GPS location

Customizable desktop dashboard feature that provides notifications from previous days, drone status, drone updates, and current flightpath settings.
