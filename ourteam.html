Meet the Team!

Benjamin Seleb

CEO & Co-Founder

Ben graduated with his bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Georgia Tech in the spring of 2020, and is continuing on to pursue a PhD in Robotics. He is the team’s current CEO and resident cow whisperer. Ben heads project management and customer discovery, and uses his engineering background to assist in system design and testing. Outside of cattle, he’s primarily interested in the development of new technology to benefit wildlife conservation.

Nick Flint

CTO & Co-Founder

Nick snagged his BS in Electrical Engineering from Yeorgia Yech in the fun-filled circus that was Spring 2020. He brings to the team a menagerie of diverse technical experience of applied hardware design in aerospace, defense, and academic research spaces. He eagerly awaits his position of cow whisperer understudy but also serves as the team CTO, where he finally answers the question of “how much hair must I lose before this drone will fly?” Outside of Ben’s cattle, he’s primarily interested in the future frontiers of tech, including personal semiconductor research and the future of aerospace products.
